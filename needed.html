Needed Final Grade Calculator

Final Grade Calculator

A final grade calculator which has two functions. The first is a needed final grade calculator where you enter your current grade, how much your final is worth and the grade you want, and it returns what you need to score on the final to get that grade. The second is a predicted final grade calculator where you enter your current grade, how much your final is worth and the grade you think you will get on the final, and it returns what your grade will be if you get that score.
