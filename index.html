High School GPA Calculator

A high school GPA calculator that takes in high school grades and returns the calculated GPA. It has a lot of optionality which allows it to cover most systems of GPA calculation that high schools use, but there might be some that are incompatible.

Needed Final Grade Calculator

A needed final grade calculator which takes in your grade, how much your final is worth and what grade you want to have in the class and returns what grade you need to get on the final exam.

Predicted Final Grade Calculator

A predicted final grade calculator which takes in your grade, how much your final is worth and what grade you think you'll get on the final and returns what your final grade will be if you get that score.
